Accountability: About This Data Set

The title of the dataset is "Top 5 importers of US soybeans." It contains quite a bit more than a list of five countries though. It shows how many metric tons of soybeans are being exported to different countries over time. It also rank orders them by amount. For each country there is a separate worksheet with more detailed data on that country's activity over time.

Where did this data come from?

The data was found while browsing data.gov. The specific website for it is: https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/grain-transportation-report-table-14. If you look at the xlsx file it contains numerous worksheets. I took the data for this assignment from the "China" worksheet. Incidentally, China is the top importer of US soybeans.

Who has access to it?

It is a public dataset created by the U.S. Department of Agriculture so anybody who wants to look has access to it.
